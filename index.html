Privacy Policy

For: Offline PDF Merge App
Effective date: 2026-03-01

Summary
  • This app processes PDFs on your device only.
  • We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.
  • We do not upload your files to any server.

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles information. If you have questions, contact: sangman.studio@gmail.com.

2) What information the app handles

2.1 Files you choose (PDF documents)

The app can access PDF files only when you select them using Android’s system file picker (Storage Access Framework). The app reads the selected PDFs to merge them and writes the merged PDF to a location you choose.

2.2 Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID, IMEI, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics, crash analytics, or tracking events
  • Contents of your PDFs to any remote server

3) How your information is used

The app uses the files you select strictly to provide the app’s core functionality (merging PDFs) and to show you the merge result (success/failure and saved location details).

4) Where processing happens

All processing happens locally on your device. The app does not operate its own backend server and does not upload your PDFs or metadata to any external service.

5) Sharing and disclosure

We do not share your data with third parties because we do not collect or transmit it. The app does not include advertising SDKs or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.

6) Permissions

The app uses Android’s system file picker so you can choose PDFs and choose where to save the merged file. This approach is designed to minimize permissions and limit access to only the files you select.

7) Data retention

The app does not maintain a remote account or server-side storage. The PDFs you select remain on your device/storage. The merged output file is saved only to the location you choose. Any temporary in-memory processing data exists only during processing and is not intentionally stored by the app.

8) Security

We take reasonable measures within the app design to reduce risk (for example, local-only processing and minimal permissions). However, no method of storage or processing is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9) Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided personal information to us (unlikely because we do not collect it), please contact us and we will address the concern.

10) International users

The app operates locally on your device and does not transfer your files to our servers. Your local device and any storage provider you use (for example, a document provider integrated into Android) may have their own practices.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the Effective date above.

12) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: sangman.studio@gmail.com

본 앱은 사용자의 개인정보를 수집하거나 저장하거나 전송하지 않습니다.

1.1 사용자가 선택한 파일(PDF 문서)

앱은 Android 시스템 파일 선택기(Storage Access Framework)를 통해 사용자가 직접 선택한 PDF 파일에 한해 접근할 수 있습니다. 선택된 파일은 병합 기능 수행을 위해 기기 내에서만 읽혀지며, 사용자가 지정한 위치에 병합된 결과 파일이 저장됩니다.

1.2 수집하지 않는 정보

본 앱은 다음 정보를 수집하지 않습니다:

  • 이름, 이메일 주소, 전화번호 등 개인식별정보
  • 광고 ID, 기기 식별자(IMEI 등)
  • 위치 정보
  • 사용 통계 데이터
  • 크래시 분석 데이터
  • PDF 파일의 내용 또는 메타데이터를 외부 서버로 전송

2. 정보의 이용 목적

사용자가 선택한 PDF 파일은 오직 다음 목적을 위해 사용됩니다:
